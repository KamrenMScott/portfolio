CAMPAIGN

I created the campaign below to run across Instagram, Tumblr, Facebook, and Twitter. The concept was to take moving the website from Tumblr to its own domain and relating it to something college students understand. Moving is a huge part of college life - leaving their parents' house for the dorms or out of the dorms to their first apartment. It's an exciting time for students, and I wanted to evoke some of the same emotions with this campaign.

I created the concept, wrote all of the copy, designed the graphics, and scheduled them using Hootsuite.

I ran each graphic with a call to action for the website, college hashtags, and concise information about the site.

BLOG

I created the CollegeSmarts blog with three goals 1.) to create more pages of the website and increase domain authority and 2.) to create original content to share on social media and 3.) to occasionally publish promotional content.

I created and updated the schedule, which consisted of running a blog post every Tuesday and Thursday at 1 PM. This was a strategic time. Tuesday and Thursday are statistically the most productive days, and they are also the days people are most likely to read blogs. However, scheduling a post at 1 PM isn't the standard. Usually blog posts run in the morning around 10 AM, but we found our users interacted with content most from noon to 2 PM. We attributed this to breaks between classes.
