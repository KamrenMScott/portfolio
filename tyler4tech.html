Tyler4Tech is a non-profit organization attempting to bring tech professionals and businesses to Tyler.

I worked as a contract marketing consultant to create Tyler4Tech’s brand and marketing collateral.

  • Created Tyler4Tech logo to coincide with the four pillars of the organization’s mission and to align with the organization’s desire to emphasize that Tyler is the rose capital of the world
  • Designed and developed the Tyler4Tech website, which is responsive and cross-browser compatible
  • Launched Tyler4Tech’s social media presence with custom graphics
  • Created a variety of print collateral for Tyler4Tech including brochures, invitations, postcards, staff badges, t-shirts, business cards, and even custom exhibit booth graphics for SXSW
