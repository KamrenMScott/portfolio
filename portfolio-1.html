I managed and analyzed Space Center Houston’s digital presence in areas including web design, social media, SEO, digital advertising, photography, and videography.
  • Established the center’s holistic digital media strategy
  • Increased social media followers by 800% (exceeding 300,000) and doubled annual impressions (exceeding 28 million)
  • Completed an award-winning website redesign that included a bright, clean interface as well as the addition of a blog and dynamic event calendar
  • Made improvements to the content, design, and UI of the website, resulting in double the online revenue ($14 million/year)
  • Developed programmatic, multi-channel ad campaigns with $1M+ total ad spend resulting in an 85% increase in sessions (4 million/year) and up to a 200% ROI
  • Implemented SEO tactics resulting in an 80% increase in the number of pages indexed by search engines and a 15% increase in organic search traffic
  • Spearheaded the development and marketing of a successful Kickstarter campaign for the restoration of Apollo Mission Control, raising over $500,000
