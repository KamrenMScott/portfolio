Times Square Vapor Lounge

Times Square Vapor Lounge is an e-cigarette retailer in Texas.

Marketing
Web & Print Design

After several vapor lounges popped up in the area, Times Square Vapor Lounge needed to develop a unique and recognizable identity that created brand awareness and loyalty.

Working from the red and black color scheme requested by the client, I created the logo for multiple mediums that would meet current and future needs of the company. The V-shape in the M is emphasized to become an iconic symbol for the vapor lounge. I created digital and print collateral that invokes immediate brand association.

I also created items including magazine and web ads, social media graphics, and posters.
